About Me

I live in Dhaka, Bangladesh, and my academic journey started at Willes Little Flower School & College, where I completed my O-Levels and A-Levels back in 2019. Later, I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering at North South University in Dec 2024. My 4 years at NSU were mostly spent on coding, but when I wasn’t buried in code, you would find me watching a Barça game—I rarely miss one!

If you ask me what I’m most passionate about academically, the answer is simple: Machine Learning. There’s something fascinating about training models to handle tasks we do every day, automating processes, and making life a little easier. I love the idea of feeding raw data to an intelligent system and watching it learn, adapt, and even outperform human efforts in certain tasks.

Whether it’s optimizing AI-powered healthcare solutions, building autonomous decision-making models, or working on ASR-based CAPTCHA recognition, I enjoy solving complex problems through AI. For me, it's all about making technology more intuitive, efficient, and accessible.
